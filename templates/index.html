Overview

Our Motorhome park was opened 12 months ago and in that short space of time has won numerous awards from tourism and International motorhome bodies. We are famous for our warm welcome and will help in any possible to make your stay as enjoyable as possible. The motorhome park is situated in a beautiful part of the Wild Atlantic mid way between Lahinch and Spanish Point. Our office opening hours are between 8am and 6pm daily, for earlier or later arrivals please call and special arrangements can be facilitated.

Directions

Head to Ennis and then follow signs for Miltown Malbay / Spanish Point. In Spanish Point take coast road towards lahinch and you will find us approx 5 km outside Spanish Point. Any problems please contact us.

Transport

We can provide a shuttle service to nearby towns and have agreements with a number of local bars and restaurants. For trips further afield please download the FreeNow app for taxis or contact Tom's Taxis on 089 7774556.
